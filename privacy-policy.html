Privacy Policy

Ludovico Zitelli privacy policy last update on 12/07/2022

This Privacy Policy describes the ways in which your personal data are collected, used and disseminated when you visit ludovicozitelli.com (the "Site").


COLLECTION OF PERSONAL DATA

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information on your device such as browser data to determine the type of platform from which you visit the Site (desktop or mobile) and your IP address (anonymously) to determine the city and country from which you visit the Site. In addition, as you browse the Site we collect specific information about web pages and how you interact with the Site. We will define this automatically collected information as "Device Information".

We collect Device Information with the following technologies:

  • ”Cookies” are data files that are placed on your device or computer, and often include an anonymous unique identification code.
  • “Tags” and “pixels” are electronic files used to record data on how you browse the Site.

HOW DO WE USE YOUR PERSONAL DATA?

We use the Device Information collected to improve and optimize our Site (for example, by generating analytical data on how visitors navigate and interact with the Site).

DISCLOSURE OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA

We do not share your Personal Data with any third party company. However, we may disclose your Personal Data to third parties to comply with applicable laws and regulations or in order to protect our rights.

Please note that your data is saved on a secure server located in a country of the European Union

DO NOT TRACK

We respect your privacy and in case you activated "Do Not Track" option from your browser we do not automatically collect Device Information.

YOUR RIGHTS

If you are located in Europe, you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you and to request that to be updated, corrected or removed. If you wish to exercise this right, please contact us using the contact informations below.

DATA STORAGE

We keep your personal data in our records until you ask us to delete that information.

CHANGES

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect changes to our procedures, or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.
